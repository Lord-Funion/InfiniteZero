▼ What is day Zero?

The DMCA attacking us at some point is inevitable. When the DMCA attacks us, our P-NP instance will definitely go down. Prodigy hacks would be dead... but Infinite Zero is the fix.


▼ How will Infinite Zero make Prodigy hacks invincible?

On day Zero, we'll have to shut down our instances of P-NP, right? That means Prodigy hacks will be dead... right?

No, we have a way to fix this. That's what Infinite Zero is for. PHEx will not rely on a single default P-NP domain, instead it will use Zero-Server to get a P-NP domain, and use the domain for Prodigy hacks.


▼ Does Infinite Zero have any flaws?

Yes and no.

Think of infinitezero.net as the Pirate Bay. It doesn't actually have any hacks, copies of Prodigy's code, P-NP instances, or anything else. It just tells PHEx (your client) which domain to use for P-NP.


▼ Where can I find the code to the Infinite Zero Server?

You can find our code on GitHub: ProdigyPNP/InfiniteZero


▼ So... where's the actual Zero Server?

Most URLs on infinitezero.net will work. For example, I can do: infinitezero.net/domain, and tada! There's the P-NP URL! There's also an automated loader for P-NP, at infinitezero.net/eval!
